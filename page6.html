Project 1: Faux Selfies

Artist Statement

‘J.J.’ is my alter ego for this project, and he really misses the 90s. He’s a goth loving, egotistical, fashion inspired, guitar player from L.A. He works in the restaurant industry, and often struggles with addiction. J.J. often thinks of himself as superior to others; nobody gets him, it’s not that he’s out of touch. He often keeps to himself, and doesn’t handle large crowds very well. But even when things appear blissful, there’s always something looming over him, a frustration with the world moving on without him. To embody this idea I used rock stars (and actors of them) who peaked in the 80s-90s, to represent J.J. The only place he gets any peace is in the kitchen, where he can forget about the rest of the modern world. A full list of his traits can be viewed here.
