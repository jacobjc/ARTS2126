Project 2: Exquisite Corpse Soundscape

Artist Statement
I set out to create a soundscape that embodied a drug trip gone wrong. My goal was to severely amplify the effects of smoking pot, providing an experience that may make the listener feel a bit weird and confused. The track begins with an individual taking what they presume to be normal drugs - but that notion changes quickly. I chose to break the soundscape up into different sections to showcase the effect throughout different parts of the stoner’s day. This is done through a consistent audible effect of an intercut bong rip. Each part should feel distinct from each other, but not immediately identifiable for what it represents in real life. A wide variety of effects were used to distort reality, such as splicing speech, reversing sounds, and much more. The piece is finished with a sped up recap of the entire timeline, completed with a final exhale. This signifies the end of the trip, and a return to normalcy.
